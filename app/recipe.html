Weekly Meal Prep Formula

Sofia K. @sofiak

A flexible, repeatable framework for 4–5 days of lunches. The base is always rice and protein — the sauce is where you make it yours. Takes about 90 minutes on Sunday and saves you from sad desk lunches all week.

20 min
Prep Time
70 min
Cook Time
4
Servings
Easy
Difficulty
Ingredients
For the Base
2 cups brown rice, uncooked
1½ lb chicken breast, boneless skinless
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp kosher salt
½ tsp black pepper, freshly ground
For the Sauce
3 tbsp soy sauce low sodium
1 tbsp sesame oil, toasted
1 tbsp honey
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
½ tsp chili flakes optional
To Finish
3 green onions, thinly sliced
1 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
1 lime, cut into wedges
Tools
Large skillet or wok (12 inch)
Rice cooker or medium saucepan with lid
4 meal prep containers
Cutting board and chef's knife
Small mixing bowl
Measuring cups and spoons
Directions
Prep
01

Rinse the brown rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook according to package instructions — typically a 1:2 ratio with water, brought to a boil then simmered covered for 40–45 minutes. Rinsing removes excess starch and gives you fluffier, less clumpy rice.

02

While the rice cooks, slice the chicken breast against the grain into strips roughly ½ inch thick. Pat dry with paper towels — this is the key to a good sear, not a steam.

03

In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, garlic, ginger, and chili flakes until the honey is fully dissolved. Set aside.

Cook
04

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until it shimmers and begins to smoke lightly. Season the chicken strips with salt and pepper.

05

Add the chicken in a single layer — don't crowd the pan. Cook undisturbed for 3–4 minutes until a golden crust forms on the bottom. Flip and cook another 2–3 minutes until cooked through. Work in two batches if needed. Crowding causes steaming, not browning.

06

Reduce heat to medium-low. Pour the sauce over the chicken and toss to coat. Cook for 1–2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and clings to the meat. Remove from heat.

Assemble
07

Fluff the cooked rice with a fork and divide evenly across 4 meal prep containers, filling roughly half of each container.

08

Arrange the sauced chicken alongside the rice. Spoon any remaining sauce from the pan over the top.

09

Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Add a lime wedge to each container — a squeeze just before eating brightens everything up. Seal and refrigerate for up to 5 days. Let containers cool to room temperature before sealing to prevent condensation.
